## 🔬 Research

My research sits at the intersection of **Knowledge Representation**, **NLP**, and **AI for Public Health**, with a focus on making AI systems explainable, interoperable, and deployable in resource-constrained environments.

| Area | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **Ontology Engineering** | Alignment, reasoning, One Health knowledge graphs |
| **Clinical NLP** | Information extraction from medical free-text, ICD-10/ATC coding |
| **Explainable AI (XAI)** | Interpretability of ML models for health applications |
| **Low-resource AI** | African languages, on-premises LLMs, privacy-preserving systems |
